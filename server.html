Building your customer server – how it works

Midedge Digital is your one-stop shop for building custom-servers to fuel your business applications and meet ongoing storage requirements. Simply go to build server, and start choosing the components you need to come up with the ultimate server hardware configuration. Not a bad way to put your competitors’ servers to shame we think!

Simply choose your preferred hardware components and slap on any upgrades you need for single or dual processors, along with customized DIMM memory slots, blazing fast SSDs, network interface cards, rail kits, RAID controllers, and what have you. Once you’re done, just go to the checkout page and your server will arrive at your doorstep within 48 hours. After you’re done handpicking the right server hardware, don’t forget to compliment it with a variety of software options, all from trusted brands including Microsoft and VMware. If you need any help selecting the right server components or software, please let us know.
